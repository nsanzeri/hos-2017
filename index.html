You are the reason Hooked On Sonics exists

You have read that right. Hooked On Sonics isn't the biggest band in Chicago, just one of the busiest and the best - thanks to you! We love watching all of you have the time of your lives when we play. An average of 80 shows a year for the last 11 years is proof of our love for what we do. Many of you we play for year after year. The quality of our sound is further proof of the mountain of work we put into every detail - to bring you our very best - and what has made us, for many of you, your favorite band. Of course we don't play every song on your ipod - that is impossible. We are constantly curating the sweet spot of what we love and are inspired to play, and what will get people out on the floor and keep them there all night. But you are not here because you want a formulaic, cookie-cutter band. You want the down to earth, inspired, surprising and always fun band that makes any night truly memorable - you want Hooked On Sonics!
